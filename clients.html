Clients

Anyone can become a client. Sign up is free of charge. With a Beep client account you can request help at any time, anywhere. You are only charged for the times that you requested help.

Rates:

Rates are based on supply and demand. To guarantee help, even for peak demand times we have to adjust rates dynamically. If demand is high rates will rise, if demand is low rates will drop.

type demand status rate
starting rate normal € 3.50
price / minute normal € 0,38
starting rate high € 6.50
price per minute high € 0.54

You will always be notified which rates apply
